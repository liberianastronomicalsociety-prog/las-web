Your Journey to the Stars Starts Here

Becoming a member of the Liberian Astronomical Society is a step-by-step journey designed to welcome passionate stargazers like you. Follow these simple steps to join our mission of unlocking the cosmos from Liberia.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and a password to complete the registration form.

2

Pass the Assessment

Take a 15-question assessment and score at least 8 to proceed to the next stage.

3

Interview with an Admin

We'll schedule an interview with an LAS administrator to get to know you better.

4

Complete Official Form & Dues

Upon approval, you will receive an official form and pay a one-time newcomer due of $5 USD.

5

Receive Your Official Letter

Once complete, you will receive your official membership letter via your portal.

School Membership Benefits

Any schools or universities interested in joining the Liberia Astronomical Society (LAS) are under this category.

🎉 Free Admission Policy: Free admission is granted to members of high school and university science clubs.

  1. 1. Direct Outreach & Programs
    1. a. School Visits and Workshops: The LAS frequently organizes Outreach Programs and Hands-on Workshops at schools and universities, which your institution would be prioritized to receive.
    2. b. Star Gazing Events: Hosting or co-hosting Star Gazing events on your campus for your students, making astronomy accessible and exciting.
  2. 2. Educational & Research Opportunities
    1. a. Student and Faculty Participation in Projects: Access to participate in LAS initiatives like the Citizen Science Project (e.g., asteroid hunting campaigns), allowing students to contribute to real-world astronomical research.
    2. b. Curriculum Enhancement: Gaining access to resources and expertise to help faculty integrate practical astronomy and STEM activities into the curriculum.
    3. c. Guest Speakers/Seminars: Opportunities to have LAS members or invited guests present at your institution for Conferences/Seminars.
  3. 3. Networking & Recognition
    1. a. Collaboration: A platform to establish and foster partnerships with other university science clubs and international partners.
    2. b. Promotion: The opportunity for your school to be recognized and promoted as an active member supporting STEM and astronomy education in Liberia.
    3. c. Advocacy: Joining the collective voice that advocates for astronomy to be offered as a degree course at universities in Liberia, which helps elevate science education standards.
  4. 4. Skills Development
    1. a. Training: Access to specialized training and technical guidance, such as the Astrophotography Training or that LAS conducts.
    2. b. Leadership Development: Opportunities for your students to take on roles in organizing joint activities, fostering leadership and organizational skills.

LAS & SGAC Liberia

The Liberian Astronomical Society serves as the official coordinating office for the Space Generation Advisory Council (SGAC) in Liberia. Through this role, LAS connects Liberian students and young professionals to global opportunities in space science, policy, and international collaboration.

Asteroid Hunting Research Program

Through our collaboration with the International Astronomical Search Collaboration (IASC) and PACSe-Lab, the Liberian Astronomical Society is actively engaged in asteroid hunting research. This initiative enables students and young researchers in Liberia to contribute to real astronomical discoveries using real telescope data.

Interested in joining our asteroid hunting team? We welcome passionate students and aspiring researchers to be part of this global scientific effort.
