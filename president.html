About the President

Daniel Ayorinde Obajemu

Founder & President, Liberian Astronomical Society

Daniel A. Obajemu is a visionary in the field of astronomy, dedicated to advancing scientific education in Africa, specifically Liberia. As the founder and President of the Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS), he established Liberia's first astronomical society to promote space science and inspire youth. He also serves as a Research Analyst at the Pan-African Citizen Science e-Laboratory and was appointed Committee Chair for the PACS e-Lab Committee, an initiative under the African Astronomical Society (AfAS).

Honours & Awards

  • Liberia Academic Excellence Award 4.0 - Educate the NextGen / Ministry of Education, Liberia (2024)
  • IAAC's National Award & Bronze Honor - International Astronomy & Astrophysics Competition (2023)
