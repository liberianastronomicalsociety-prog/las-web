About the President

Daniel Ayorinde Obajemu, leading LAS to inspire Liberia's next generation of space scientists and astronomers.

Daniel Ayorinde Obajemu

Founder & President, Liberian Astronomical Society

Daniel A. Obajemu is a visionary in astronomy, dedicated to advancing scientific education in Liberia and Africa. He founded LAS to promote space science and inspire youth.

Honours & Awards

  • Liberia Academic Excellence Award 3.0 - Educate the NextGen / Ministry of Education, Liberia (2024)
  • IAAC's National Award & Bronze Honor - International Astronomy & Astrophysics Competition (2023)
