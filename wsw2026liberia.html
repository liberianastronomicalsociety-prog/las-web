Why it matters

Building Liberia’s next generation of scientists and innovators

The programme uses astronomy and space science as an entry point into STEM learning, helping students connect classroom knowledge with real-world technology, careers and national development.

🚀

Rocket Revolution

Interactive sessions on rockets, satellites, space exploration and the technologies shaping the modern world.

🔭

Astronomy in Action

Hands-on activities, sky exploration, telescope demonstrations and opportunities to work with real astronomy tools.

🎓

STEM Careers

Career talks and mentoring designed to help students imagine themselves as scientists, engineers, researchers and innovators.

Rocket Revolution & Closing

Schools & participation
