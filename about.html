Our Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To spread astronomy education across Liberia.

Our Mission

  • Introduce astronomy to the public and spark curiosity.
  • Engage young Liberians through hands-on activities.
  • Advocate for astronomy as a university degree course.
  • Foster partnerships with science clubs nationwide.
