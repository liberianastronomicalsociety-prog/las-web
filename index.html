Liberian Astronomical Society

"Unlocking the cosmos from Liberia."

The Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded on April 12, 2025, with the aim of spreading astronomy education across Liberia and foster a national STEM culture in the country. We are proud to be the first astronomical sector ever recorded in Liberia's history.

About the President

Daniel Ayorinde Obajemu

Founder & President, Liberian Astronomical Society

Daniel A. Obajemu is a visionary in the field of astronomy, dedicated to advancing scientific education in Africa, specifically Liberia. As the founder and President of the Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS), he has established Liberia's first astronomical society to promote space science. Daniel also serves as a Research Analyst at the Pan-African Citizen Science e-Laboratory and was appointed Committee Chair for the PACS e-Lab Committee, an initiative under the African Astronomical Society (AfAS). His educational background includes a High School Diploma and Vocational Certificate from Monrovia College & Industrial Training School.

Your Journey to the Stars Starts Here

Becoming a member of the Liberian Astronomical Society is a step-by-step journey designed to welcome passionate stargazers like you. Follow these simple steps to join our mission of unlocking the cosmos from Liberia.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and a password to complete the registration form.

2

Pass the Assessment

Take a 15-question assessment and score at least 8 to proceed to the next stage.

3

Interview with an Admin

We'll schedule an interview with an LAS administrator to get to know you better.

4

Complete Official Form & Dues

Upon approval, you will receive an official form and pay a one-time newcomer due of $5 USD.

5

Receive Your Official Letter

Once complete, you will receive your official membership letter via your portal.
