Liberian Astronomical Society

"Unlocking the cosmos from Liberia."

The Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded on April 2025, with the aim of spreading astronomy education across the country. We are proud to be the first astronomical sector ever recorded in Liberia's history.

About the President

Daniel Ayorinde Obajemu

Founder & President, Liberian Astronomical Society

Daniel A. Obajemu is a visionary in the field of astronomy, dedicated to advancing scientific education in Africa, specifically Liberia. As the founder and President of the Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS), he has established Liberia's first astronomical society to promote space science. Daniel also serves as a Research Analyst at the Pan-African Citizen Science e-Laboratory and was appointed Committee Chair for the PACS e-Lab Committee, an initiative under the African Astronomical Society (AfAS). His educational background includes a High School Diploma and Vocational Certificate from Monrovia College & Industrial Training School.
