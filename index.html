Liberian Astronomical Society

"Unlocking the cosmos from Liberia."

The Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS) is a non-governmental organization (NGO) founded on April 12, 2025, with the aim of spreading astronomy education across Liberia and foster a national STEM culture in the country. We are proud to be the first astronomical sector ever recorded in Liberia's history.

A Message from the President

Greetings from the Liberian Astronomical Society!

As a growing scientific community, LAS is dedicated to igniting a national passion for astronomy and fostering scientific literacy across Liberia. We are actively building a robust national network of astronomy education, collaborating with communities and partners to ensure every young Liberian has hands-on access to the wonders of the universe. We don't just teach science; by investing in direct mentorship and pioneering STEM initiatives, we are empowering the next generation of innovators who will define Liberia's future in the global space and technology frontier.

Together, let's shape a stronger scientific future for our country - Liberia.

— Daniel Obajemu

President, Liberian Astronomical Society (LAS)
